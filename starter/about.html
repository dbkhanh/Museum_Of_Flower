ABOUT/US

OUR MISSION

The Museum of Flowers is dedicated to celebrating the timeless beauty and diversity of floral arrangements. Our mission is to inspire and educate visitors by showcasing the art of floral design from different cultures and eras, while promoting an appreciation for the natural world.

OUR HISTORY

Founded in 2024, the Museum of Flowers has grown from a small gallery to a renowned institution. Our exhibits feature a vast collection of floral arrangements, historical artifacts, and contemporary designs that highlight the significance of flowers in art, culture, and everyday life.

OUR TEAM

Our team consists of passionate florists, historians, and educators who are committed to bringing the beauty and knowledge of flowers to the public. With diverse backgrounds and expertise, we strive to create engaging and informative exhibits and programs for all our visitors.
